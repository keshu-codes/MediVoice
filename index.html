About MediVoice

MediVoice is a smart, voice-activated medication reminder system designed to help individuals manage their medicines effortlessly using natural speech. Whether it's Aspirin at 9:30 AM or Paracetamol at 7:45 PM, just say it — and MediVoice takes care of the rest.

Powered by modern web technologies and speech recognition, this project was built with social impact in mind during the Hack the Vibe 2025 hackathon.
